Get your cooking materials ready

Clear a surface in your kitchen for you to set your stuff on. You're going to need some eggs (one to three depending on how many eggs you want), salt, pepper, & oil.

For oil, you can use liquid or spray-on, it's whatever you have available. For this demonstration, we'll be using spray on olive oil. You'll also be needing a frying pan to cook your egg on.

Heat the pan, apply oil

Just like the title says, place your frying pan on the stove top and heat it with low to mid heat. Spray oil on to the surface of the pan (if you are using liquid oil, go ahead pour a thin layer of oil onto the surface of the pan and swish it around until it is spread evenly).

Hire award winning chef and restaurant owner Gordon Ramsey to do the rest!

Hopefully you didn't use too much oil before he got there. Try your best to stay out of the way as the world-renowned master chef gets to work on your eggs.

Enjoy!
